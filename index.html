Peninsula Grand

B4

3 Bedrooms

Sleeps 6

4 Bathrooms

Fully Stocked Kitchen

Peninsula Grand is a 3 bedroom 4 bath luxury condo with 24 hour security just a 5 minute ride north of downtown Cozumel. This unit, B4, is on the 4th floor of the south building of two towers. Each of the 3 bedrooms and kitchen have ocean views; the kitchen and studio also overlook the jungle. This spacious 2400 sq ft condo with tiled floors throughout is equipped with everything you will need for a comfortable stay. The dining room seats 8. There is a 216 sq ft private balcony off the dining/living rooms that offer sweeping panoramic views of the ocean. The master bedroom also has a 127 sq ft balcony. Right downstairs you will find 300 ft of private beach with stairstep access to the ocean (great snorkeling!), 2 outdoor showers, a large heated swimming pool, 2 saunas, 2 steam rooms, a massage room (appt needed), a gym with ocean views, showers and bathrooms in the gym, a recreation room with ocean views equipped with a pool table and cable TV, and a snack bar with food service (10am - 6pm- check for cook's day off). This is the only condo complex with on site food service. Food and drinks are available poolside or on the beach.

The fully equipped kitchen includes granite counter tops, purified water, coffee maker, George Foreman grill, toaster, microwave, blender. The living room has a 42 inch cable TV and 30 DVD movies to chose from. There is a programmable safe on the premises for valuables. Free internet, free phone calls to US & Canada. Maid service included.

Watch the cruise ships pass by and gorgeous sunsets from the balcony.
